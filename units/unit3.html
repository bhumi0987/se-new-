Very Short Questions

  • What does SOLID stand for? (one word per principle)
  • Name one architectural pattern.
  • What is an API?
  • Define 'coupling' in one word.

Short Questions

  1. Explain the Single Responsibility Principle with an example.

    Classes or modules should have one reason to change; for example separate logging from business logic.

  2. Describe when to choose microservices over a monolith.

    Choose microservices for large teams, independent deployability, and when scaling parts independently is required.

  3. How does modularity improve maintainability?

    Modules isolate changes, reduce side-effects and make understanding and testing easier.

  4. Give a short example of a performance-focused design decision.

    Caching frequently-requested data at the edge to reduce latency and backend load.

MVP / High-yield Questions

  • Explain when to choose microservices over monoliths.
  • List 5 core SOLID principles and a one-line use-case for each.
  • Describe how caching improves performance with an example.
  • Give a quick checklist for designing interfaces between modules.
